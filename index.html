About Me

I am a third-year mechanical engineering student at the University of Texas at Austin, passionate about utilizing my engineering design skills to solve real-world challenges. Currently, I work as a Student Assistant at Texas Inventionworks and serve as a Structures Engineer for the Texas Rocket Engineering Lab, where I hope to share my love for innovation.

I am also a huge fan of tech and stocks. In my free time, you can find me playing volleyball with the UT men's club team or trying new foods around Austin. I also love trying new things and learning new skills, one of the main reasons I decided to make my own portfolio website. Feel free to visit the Contact Me section or send me a message!
